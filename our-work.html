Method

Every study reads the same three ways.

We present work the way we make it — in order. First the situation as we found it. Then the decisions, drafts, and deletions of the middle. Then what changed once it shipped. No screenshot without a reason.

The context.

Where the business stood, what it was up against, and the single problem worth solving first. The brief behind the brief.

The craft.

The work in between — directions explored, the one chosen, and the things removed to get there. The part most studios hide.

The outcome.

What the work left behind, measured where it can be. Quiet results count too: clarity, calm, a brand that finally fits.
