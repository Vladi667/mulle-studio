01 — Why it exists

Most companies still scale on instinct.

The numbers are scattered.

Revenue in one tool, traffic in another, leads in a third — the real picture in a spreadsheet nobody fully trusts.

Talent does mechanical work.

Capable people spending their weeks on enrichment, follow-ups and reports a machine should quietly be running.

Decisions ride on instinct.

Without an honest weekly read on what's working, the next move is a guess — and growth stays accidental.

02 — What we build

Three layers. One quiet machine.

Visibility — one source of truth.

The numbers that govern the business — revenue, pipeline, acquisition, retention — composed into one live view. No exports, no reconciling, no argument over whose figure is right.

Seven tabs and a Monday spreadsheet→one authoritative screen

Autonomy — work that runs itself.

A quiet, Claude-powered workforce that carries the mechanical load — qualifying and enriching every inbound lead, drafting and routing follow-ups, assembling reports — without a hand on the wheel.

Leads cooling in an inbox→researched, scored & routed in minutes

Counsel — a clear next move.

Each week, a concise read on what moved, what stalled, and the single experiment worth your attention. Judgment on top of the data — not another chart to interpret.

Guesswork dressed as strategy→one considered move, weekly

03 — How it works

From signal to system, in four moves.

Step01

We map where your data lives, what's done by hand, and where value leaks. One week, fixed scope, a clear verdict.

Step02

We build the single source of truth — tracking wired, dashboard composed — until every number finally agrees with itself.

Step03

Automate

We deploy the agents that carry the mechanical load: enrichment, follow-up, reporting, lifecycle — quietly, around the clock.

Step04

Compound

Each week we read the signal, run one deliberate experiment, and refine the system. Advantage that accrues.
