01 — Origin

Built on the idea that subtraction is the only loudness left.

Six years independent, trained in graphic design at École de Condé, Lyon — and one conviction that hasn't moved: design is mostly deciding what to leave out.

I work end to end, from the core of an identity to every surface it has to live on. Agencies, restaurants, small and mid-sized businesses, startups, artists, large groups — different rooms, the same discipline.

02 — Process

Four steps. No surprises.

Every engagement follows the same architecture. Fixed price up front. Fixed delivery date. The work that happens in between is craft, and that's where we get specific.

Step01

Conversation

A free 30-minute call. We listen for the shape of the problem, not just the brief. If we are not the right fit, we say so.

Step02

Proposal

One document. The scope, the price, the deliverables, the timeline. Within 24 hours of the call. No revisions of scope after signature.

Step03

Craft

We make. You see early drafts, you give honest feedback, we refine. Revision rounds are bounded; the work is not.

Step04

Hand-off

Files, source materials, full commercial licence. Post-launch support included. We stay reachable.

03 — Values

Three things we believe.

Quiet over loud.

A surface should hold weight, then disappear. We design for the second look, not the first scroll.

Fixed over flexible.

Fixed price, fixed scope, fixed timeline. Everything you should not have to worry about is decided before we start.

Restraint over reach.

If it can be removed, we remove it. The best deliverable is the one that didn't need anything else.
