01 — Why it exists

Most ad budgets buy guesswork.

Too few shots on goal.

Agencies ship two or three ads a month. The algorithm needs dozens to find what works — so the budget starves the test before it starts.

Slow and expensive.

Every creative is a shoot, a round, an invoice. By the time the ad is live, the moment it was made for has already passed.

Spend on instinct.

Budget poured into a handful of guesses, with nothing deciding in real time what to scale and what to quietly kill.
